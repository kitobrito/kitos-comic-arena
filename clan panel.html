The clan recruitement section let's you create wishlist for your clan and send invitations to retrive new members.

The clan Info section let's you customize few things about you clan to give you clan more identity or style.

The clan management section lets you maintain your members. You can create clanranks and assign these to members.

The clan avatar is an icon or logo which visualizes the clan, it is shown in the profile of a clan.

The clan invitations sections shows all the invitations you have received from clans. Accepting invitations lets you join the clan.

The clan register form lets you create a clan. You can only create your own clan when you are currently clanless.

You can always decide to leave your clan. If you leave as the Clan Leader your clan will be disbanded.
